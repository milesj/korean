The suffix `으로` can be conjugated with any noun to express the purpose of "in order to". This is
primarily used in transportation and travel, and additionally the instrument in which an action was
performed.

## Guidelines

- The suffix `으로` is used when the previous syllable ends in a consonant, except `ㄹ`.
- The suffix `로` is used when the previous syllable ends in a vowel, or `ㄹ`.

## Examples

Please speak in Korean.

```
한국어로 말하세요.
```
